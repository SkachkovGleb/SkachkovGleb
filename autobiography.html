My life before Haaga-Helia

My childhood

Howdy! My name is Gleb and I've been stumbling through life since 1990. Born in Leningrad, now St. Petersburg, I had to move all over the russia and beyond. I've spent my childhood in Kazakhstan, Siberia, a military base under moscow, Norway and finally, when i was 13, my wondering ended, when my mother settled down in Finnland, Helsinki. Moving around had it's benefits and had it's downsides, but in the end I'm happy with where I ended up.

Life in Finland

My adaptation was hard at the start. With no Finnish language, a social circle filled with other Russian speakers and a rebellious attitude, made things hard for myself. However, with time my life branched out and within a few years, I was on my way to becoming a nurse.

Study and Work life

Nursing school was alot of fun, where I made quite a few friends and in 2010, I was already working in elderly care. From there on, I was drifting from occupation to occupation. From elderly care to rehab and psych wards. For five years was working in the healthcare field, but at some point I felt overburdened and took a year, to complete my army service, where I met my wife. After the army was done, I continued my career as a nurse. While working in the Suursuo Hospital, I got familiarized with python and stumbled myself into being the responsible for the IT side of things, which was mainly me teaching my colleagues to use Outlook and making PowerPoint presentations on for the relatives. This was the spark, that ignited my interest in the technological sides of things. After a while, my resolve grew and I applied to Haaga-Helia, to the get my degree in Business Information Technology and possibly combine it with my expertise in the healthcare.

Hopes and dreams

Now I slowly, but surely make my way towards my goal. While I might be overwhelmed and tired, I love every moment of it. Hopefully my study will go smoothly and a world of possibilities await me.
